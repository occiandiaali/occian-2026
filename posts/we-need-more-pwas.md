# Why are we so obsessed with the mobile stores

Every body wants to build a **Mobile App**.  
At least, that's what I've heard since the 2010s.
My Gen-Z niece also told me that her generation only really care about mobile.

## Serious question: Why Mobile?

Mobile is lightweight, portable, and easy to format.  
It supports headings, lists, quotes, and code.
But it's also a pain-in-the-you-guess-where to distribute.
Apple and Google stores simply make life difficult. And we just accept it.
Because of distribution.
But, we already have a much better distribution outlet.
The WWW.

## Are you for real?

If the "world wide" in its name doesn't give you an idea, I don't know what will.
